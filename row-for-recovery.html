About the Event

What Is
Row for
Recovery?

Row for Recovery is a community fundraiser where teams of 6 take turns on rowers to collectively row a full marathon — 26.2 miles — together.

Every stroke raises money to keep Barbell Saves Project programs free for people in recovery. No experience needed. All fitness levels welcome.

Held at Jump Ship Phoenix, one of the valley's premier CrossFit facilities — the energy in the room is something you have to feel to believe.
