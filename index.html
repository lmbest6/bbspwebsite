Our Mission

We Believe
the Barbell Saves.

Recovery isn't just about putting down the substance — it's about building a new life with purpose, structure, and belonging. We created Barbell Saves Project because we know that movement heals, community sustains, and accountability transforms.

As the nonprofit arm of the Barbell Saves movement, we provide free-to-access programming for individuals navigating addiction recovery. No insurance required. No cost. No barriers.

We partner with licensed clinicians, peer support specialists, and community leaders to deliver an ecosystem of care that meets people exactly where they are — and walks with them forward.

Fitness. Community. Purpose.

The three pillars of a life rebuilt.

Our community in action
🏋

Movement as Medicine

Trauma-informed fitness coaching and gym access build discipline, neurological resilience, and a positive relationship with the body.

🤝

Peer-Led Support

Lived-experience peer specialists walk alongside clients — not behind a desk, but side by side on the floor and in the community.

🏠

Stable Housing

Our recovery houses provide safe, structured, sober-living environments so healing doesn't stop at 5pm.

🌐

Community Integration

Open classes, organizational partnerships, and public events remove stigma and build bridges between recovery and the broader community.

Sober Living

A Home in
Recovery

Early recovery is fragile. Having a safe, structured, and sober environment can mean the difference between relapse and lasting change. Our recovery houses are more than housing — they're communities built around shared purpose.

Residents have access to our full programming, peer support specialists, gym membership, and life-skills coaching. We hold high standards because we hold high expectations for everyone who walks through our doors.
