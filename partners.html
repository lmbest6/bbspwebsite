How We Work
With Organizations

We customize every partnership to your population and setting. Here's how we typically engage.

01

Treatment Centers & Clinical Partners

We integrate alongside your clinical team to provide movement-based programming and certified peer support as a complement to IOP, residential, or outpatient treatment.

  • Peer support co-facilitation alongside clinical staff
  • Group fitness sessions on-site or at our facility
  • Client referrals into sober living and community classes
  • Trauma-informed coaching at every session
02

Employers & Community Organizations

We bring fitness, wellness, and peer support programming to workplaces and community organizations — meeting people where they are, regardless of their recovery status.

  • Workplace fitness and wellness events
  • Mental health awareness programming
  • Community outreach and stigma reduction
  • Flexible scheduling for any setting
03

Justice & Corrections

We work directly with court-involved individuals to provide structured, pro-social programming that supports their recovery and fulfills court requirements. Our team knows the system and shows up for clients navigating it.

  • Community service support
  • Pro-social activities that fulfill court requirements
  • Court navigation assistance and advocacy
  • Drug court, probation, and reentry programming
  • Peer specialists with lived justice-involvement experience

Let's Build Something Together

Whether you're a treatment center, employer, or justice program — if you serve people who need support, there's a place for you in this network.
