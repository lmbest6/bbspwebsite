Arizona Tax Credit — QCO

Your donation could cost you nothing.

Arizona residents can donate up to $495 (single) or $987 (married) to a Qualifying Charitable Organization and receive a dollar-for-dollar credit on their state taxes. That means your gift to us is completely offset — you get every penny back at tax time.
